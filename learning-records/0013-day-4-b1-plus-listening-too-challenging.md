# Day 4 B1+ listening was too challenging

The learner attempted British Council B2 listening "A lecture about an experiment" as a B1+ stretch and scored 4/6 and 0/6, for a combined 4/12 (33%). The learner reported that many academic words were hard to understand and fill-in-the-blank questions were difficult; future B1+ listening should pre-teach academic vocabulary and start with main idea plus note-taking before blanks.
