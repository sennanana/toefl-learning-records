## Day 5 阅读第 1 题记录

- 题型：Main idea。
- 分数：2 / 3。
- 用户原文：passage is mainly about many students find it difficult to wake up early. And then, researchers make a test that if school moved start time late. Finally, the researcher find the students feel clear if School changes its start time.
- 润色版：The passage is mainly about how later school start times may help teenagers get more sleep and arrive late less often. A study found positive results at one school, but each school should consider its local conditions before changing its schedule.
- 反馈：抓到主题和研究过程，但主旨答案必须写文章真实结果 `more sleep / fewer late arrivals`，不能写文章没有说的 `feel clear`；最后补 `local conditions`。

## Day 5 阅读第 2 题记录

- 题型：Purpose。
- 分数：2 / 3。
- 用户原文：because the writer want provide a evident that changing school start time late is help students to get more sleep.
- 润色版：The writer describes the two schools to provide evidence that starting school later may help students get more sleep and arrive late less often.
- 具体问题：理解方向正确，但漏掉 School A 与 School B 的比较目的，也漏掉 `fewer late arrivals`。句子问题是 `want -> wants`、`a evident -> evidence`、`is help -> helps`，以及不能单独用 `Because` 开头。
- 解决动作：Purpose 题先写 `The writer describes [example/comparison] to show that ...`，再回原文拿两个具体结果词。

## Day 5 阅读第 3 题记录

- 题型：Detail + evidence。
- 分数：3 / 3。
- 用户原文：School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30. School B kept the old schedule.
- 润色版：School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30, while School B kept its old schedule. Evidence: “School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30.”
- 具体问题：内容正确，时间和两校对比都准确。只需加强 evidence 格式。
- 解决动作：Detail 题一律写 `Answer: ... Evidence: "..."`。

## Day 5 阅读第 4 题记录

- 题型：Vocabulary in context。
- 分数：1 / 3。
- 用户原文：Shift is closest in meaning to students can't sleep early at night.
- 润色版：In paragraph 1, `shift` is closest in meaning to `move or change to a later time`.
- 具体问题：写了后果，不是 `shift` 的词义；主语是 internal clock，不是 students；原文是 `do not feel sleepy until late`，不是 `can't sleep early`。
- 解决动作：把词直接换进原句：`internal clock shifts to a later time` -> `internal clock moves to a later time`。词义题只解释词本身。

## Day 5 阅读第 5 题记录

- 题型：Inference。
- 分数：2 / 3。
- 用户原文：The researcher say local conditions must be studied before a school changes its start time. It prove a evidence of researcher's view that moving times late is well but it must be adept to local conditions.
- 润色版：The researchers believe that later start times may be helpful, but each school should study its local conditions before making a change.
- 具体问题：抓到 `local conditions`，但第一句只是原文事实，缺少整体推断 `later starts may help, but no single rule fits every school`。语言重点：`researchers say`、`suggests`、`may be helpful`、`adapted`。
- 解决动作：Inference 题固定写 `The researchers believe that [possible benefit], but [limitation].`，先推断再引用原文事实。

## Day 5 阅读第 6 题记录

- 题型：Summary。
- 分数：2 / 3。
- 用户原文：teenagers often wake up early because they internal clock often change to a later time. School A moved its start time later helps students get a well sleepy but local conditions is matter.
- 润色版：Teenagers often find it difficult to wake up early because their internal clocks shift to a later time. When School A started later, students got more sleep and arrived late less often, but local conditions must be considered before schedules change.
- 具体问题：两条要点选对了，但把 `find it difficult to wake up early` 写成 `often wake up early`，方向相反；遗漏 `fewer late arrivals`，并把 `more sleep` 写成 `a well sleepy`。
- 解决动作：Summary 分两句写：`Cause: Teenagers ... because ...` + `Study result: School A ... , but ...`。答前检查方向词：difficult / later / more / fewer。
