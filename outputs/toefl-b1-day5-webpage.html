短学术阅读：Why Some Schools Start Later

Paragraph 1. Many teenagers find it difficult to wake up very early. This is not always because they are lazy or use their phones too much. During puberty, the body's internal clock often shifts to a later time. As a result, many teenagers do not feel sleepy until late at night. When school starts early, some students may arrive tired even if they want to learn.

Paragraph 2. Researchers in one small city studied this question. Before the change, they collected information about sleep, attendance and late arrivals at two high schools. Then, in 2022, School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30. School B kept the old schedule. After one semester, students at School A reported about 34 more minutes of sleep on school nights. The number of late arrivals also fell. At School B, researchers found no clear change.

Paragraph 3. The study does not prove that a later start time is best for every school. Bus schedules, parents' work hours and after-school activities are different in each community. However, the results suggest that schools should consider students' sleep when they make schedules. Researchers say that local conditions must be studied before a school changes its start time.

Before the change: collect data->Then: School A starts later->After one semester: compare results->Finally: consider local conditions
6 个支架词：internal clock = body timing system; shift = move/change; collect data = gather information; semester = half of a school year; tardy = late; local conditions = facts that are different in each place.

6 题练习

答题反馈记录：第 2 题 Purpose

分数：2 / 3

你的原文：because the writer want provide a evident that changing school start time late is help students to get more sleep.

润色版：The writer describes the two schools to provide evidence that starting school later may help students get more sleep and arrive late less often.

具体问题：目的方向正确，但漏掉两校比较的作用，也漏掉了 fewer late arrivals。语言上要改 `want -> wants`、`a evident -> evidence`、`is help -> helps`；单独回答不能只用 Because 开头。

解决动作：下次先写 `The writer describes [example/comparison] to show that ...`，再从原文复制两个结果词。

答题反馈记录：第 3 题 Detail + evidence

分数：3 / 3

你的原文：School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30. School B kept the old schedule.

润色版：School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30, while School B kept its old schedule. Evidence: “School A moved its start time from 7:30 to 8:30.”

具体问题：内容正确，时间和两校对比都准确。唯一可加强点是题目要求 evidence 时，要加 `Evidence:` 和一条原文句。

解决动作：Detail 题固定写 `Answer: ... Evidence: "..."`。

答题反馈记录：第 4 题 Vocabulary in context

分数：1 / 3

你的原文：Shift is closest in meaning to students can't sleep early at night.

润色版：In paragraph 1, shift is closest in meaning to move or change to a later time.

具体问题：你写了后果，不是词义。`shift` 的主语是 internal clock，不是 students；原文也不是说 students `can't` sleep early。

解决动作：词义题把词直接换进原句：`internal clock shifts to a later time` -> `internal clock moves to a later time`。只解释词，不解释后果。

答题反馈记录：第 5 题 Inference

分数：2 / 3

你的原文：The researcher say local conditions must be studied before a school changes its start time. It prove a evidence of researcher's view that moving times late is well but it must be adept to local conditions.

润色版：The researchers believe that later start times may be helpful, but each school should study its local conditions before making a change.

具体问题：你抓到 local conditions，但第一句只是原文事实，不是完整推断。要补 `later starts may help, but no single rule fits every school`。语言上改 `researchers say`、`suggests`、`may be helpful` 和 `adapted`。

解决动作：Inference 题固定写 `The researchers believe that [possible benefit], but [limitation].`。先写推断，再用一条原文事实支持。

答题反馈记录：第 6 题 Summary

分数：2 / 3

你的原文：teenagers often wake up early because they internal clock often change to a later time. School A moved its start time later helps students get a well sleepy but local conditions is matter.

润色版：Teenagers often find it difficult to wake up early because their internal clocks shift to a later time. When School A started later, students got more sleep and arrived late less often, but local conditions must be considered before schedules change.

具体问题：两条要点选对了，但第一句把事实方向写反：文章说 `find it difficult to wake up early`，不是 `often wake up early`。第二句要补 `fewer late arrivals`，并把 `a well sleepy` 改成 `more sleep`。

解决动作：Summary 写两句：`Cause: Teenagers ... because ...` + `Study result: School A ... , but ...`。每句只放一个完整事实，并检查方向词 `difficult / later / more / fewer`。

  5. They support studying later starts, but do not believe one rule fits every school.
