# Mission: TOEFL B1 Daily Study Plan

## Why
User wants a clear list-style TOEFL study plan that can be followed every day for 2 hours. Current training should use Sprout B1 native-speaker blog/listening input plus B1-level listening and reading exercises, then slowly bridge toward TOEFL academic tasks.

## Success looks like
- Follow a daily 120-minute checklist without deciding what to do each day.
- Complete Sprout B1 listening, B1 listening exercises, B1 reading exercises, and an error log each study day.
- After four weeks, feel ready to add more TOEFL-style reading/listening and take an official ETS sample test.

## Constraints
- Study time: 2 hours per day.
- Format: checklist/list style.
- Current material level: around CEFR B1.
- Must include Sprout.
- Exact TOEFL test date and target score are not known yet.

## Out of scope
- Full paid-course selection.
- Heavy grammar course.
- Full speaking/writing plan, except short summaries that support listening and reading.
