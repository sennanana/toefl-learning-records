# TOEFL B1 Study Plan Resources

## Knowledge

- [ETS: TOEFL iBT Test Content](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/about/content.html)
  Official current TOEFL iBT structure. Use for section names, item counts, timing, and scoring assumptions.
- [ETS: TOEFL iBT Reading Section](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/about/content/reading.html)
  Official current reading task description. Use to align reading practice with Complete the Words, Read in Daily Life, and Read an Academic Passage.
- [ETS: TOEFL iBT Test Resources](https://www.ets.org/toefl/test-takers/ibt/about.html)
  Official hub with sample test and preparation resources. Use when moving from B1 practice to TOEFL-format practice.
- [ETS: TOEFL iBT Sample Test](https://www.in.ets.org/sample-toefl-test.html)
  Official 40-minute self-paced sample with authentic past questions from all four skills. Use after the B1 foundation cycle.
- [Council of Europe: CEFR Global Scale B1](https://www.coe.int/en/web/common-european-framework-reference-languages/table-1-cefr-3.3-common-reference-levels-global-scale)
  Official CEFR descriptor source. Use to define what B1 listening/reading can reasonably mean.
- [British Council LearnEnglish: B1 Listening](https://learnenglish.britishcouncil.org/free-resources/listening/b1)
  B1 listening lessons with preparation, audio, and comprehension tasks. Use for daily listening exercises.
- [British Council LearnEnglish: B1 Reading](https://learnenglish.britishcouncil.org/free-resources/reading/b1)
  B1 reading lessons with preparation, text, and comprehension tasks. Use mainly for support work, not as the main reading path once TOEFL-style short academic passages are available.
- [Sprout Language](https://sproutlanguage.com/)
  Immersion video platform with levelled English videos and native accents. Use for the daily native-speaker input block.
- [VOA Learning English: Radio Programs](https://learningenglish.voanews.com/radio/programs)
  Use Science & Technology and Education reports for B1+/early-TOEFL academic bridge reading. VOA Learning English uses limited vocabulary, short sentences, and slower audio.
- [Breaking News English: Level 3 sample science lesson](https://mail.breakingnewsenglish.com/1608/160810-reading.pdf)
  Use Level 3 science, health, education, and social-science lessons when VOA texts are too easy. Keep only the article and 5-6 TOEFL-style questions; do not do every worksheet task.
- [ReadTheory](https://readtheory.org/homepage/)
  Use the adaptive pretest and then 1 passage per week for a difficulty check. It adapts passages and questions to the learner's current level.
